The Library has automated its functions to increase efficiency in routine activities and enable easy search of the bibliographic details of the holdings. It uses wi-fi technology to provide access to online resources from the departments in both the college and the hospital. The Library has also provide service users of the Central Library to find the full text articles available in print and online subscription. The Library conducts regular hands-on training which helps the users to run searches on their own to find authentic and accurate information accessible through Internet.
