Admission

We offer 3-year Diploma Courses for the following Departments:

  • Computer Science Engineering - 60 Seats
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering - 60 Seats
  • Mechanical Engineering - 60 Seats
  • Civil Engineering - 60 Seats

Requirements:

  • Should have passed SSLC
  • Should have studied in Karnataka for at least 5 years or obtain Eligibility Certificate from DTE, Bangalore for Non-Karnataka students/SSLC other than State Board
  • Lateral Entry to II Year:
    • For ITI Students (2 years trade)
    • Students Passes in PUC with Science

Application Requirements

  • The application form is to be collected from the office on payment of Rs. 200.
  • The filled-in application form is to be submitted along with the following original documents:
    • SSLC Marks card
    • Transfer Certificate
    • Medical Certificate
    • Study certificate (5 years of study in Karnataka state from 1st to 10th standard)
